About TROH Scholarship.

TROH Scholarships are offered to High School, Higher Secondary/+2 Students, Technical and Professional College Students, who have overcome the adversity of their familyâ€™s circumstances and showed excellence in their academic records until recently.

Scholarship Programs.

TROH Offers Scholarship at three different Levels:

1. School Education

2. College Education

3. Professional Course Education

Criteria for Selection :

In an academic year, students selected to each level will be benefited according to the custom tailored scholarship schemes. Following are the eligibility criterion for applying for TROH Scholarship.

1. The students applying for the merit scholarship must have 75% or above marks in their examinations

2. TROH must have recommendation letters from the class teacher and the head of the institution.

3. It is important to send TROH a copy of their mark list when they receive their mark sheets.

4. The completed application [ See Downloads ] will be submitted to TROH. All applications will be reviewed and the final decision will be made by TROH Team.

5. The scholarship is restricted to residents of Kerala,India.

Follow Up :

It is the students responsibility to update their time to time results of their all exams ,to the TROH team.Students should keep a minimum of 75% ( 'A' grade ) mark in all exams to continue the Merit Scholarship.

General Instructions:

1. TROH merit scholarship must be used for educational purposes only(like uniform,fees,books etc.). TROH is not sponsoring any children,only supporting them through the merit scholarships.

2. The educational support has no affiliation with or supports, any specific religion,race or gender.We provide equal opportunity for all the students from Kerala, India.

3. TROH will keep the confidentiality of all the students.

4. All applications will be reviewed by the TROH team and the final decision will be made by TROH team.
