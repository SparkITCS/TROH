Read More About Us

TROH scholarship for students in need!

The Rays of Hope(TROH) is a dream project developed by a group of like-minded individuals from India and the United States. Our goal is to provide scholarships, to ambitious students from low socioeconomic backgrounds. Through our actions, we hope to encourage these to strive for a better future and see that the world is a beautiful place. We feel that by doing this, these candidates will return the favor to others that are less fortunate than they are.

Our Mission and Vision

To improve the educational lives of students in need through emotional support, scholarships and continued assessment of their academic progress. To provide students with support to continue their professional education in the hope that they will, in turn , provide to other less fortunate children.
